Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon - Colorado River gorge in the US state of Arizona, across the Colorado Plateau. Lee's Ferry is considered the beginning of the canyon, while the end of the canyon is in the Grand Wash Cliff area approximately 20 km from the beginning of Lake Mead. The distance between these points, measured along the Colorado River, is 446 km. The deepest part of the Grand Canyon is Granite Gorge, where the deepest point lies below the rim by 1,857 m. The width of the canyon ranges from about 800 m to 29 km at its widest point. It is the largest gorge in the world (but not the deepest). This area is a national park - Grand Canyon National Park.
